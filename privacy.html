Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

AAFC (Aesthetic Archive for Clinics) is operated by Atilla Cengiz. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Data We Collect

AAFC collects the following data that you provide:

  • Patient names, birth dates, phone numbers, and cities
  • Visit records and clinical notes
  • Patient photos and documents (treatment cards, consent forms, product barcodes)
  • Clinic information (name, logo, doctor name, address)
  • User account information (email, name, role)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide patient archiving and management features
  • To generate before/after collages and PDF reports
  • To enable secure cloud backup and restore
  • To manage clinic users and access permissions

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Only authorized clinic users can access clinic data. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at support@aafclinic.com.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Cloud database and storage
  • Resend — Email delivery

6. Children Privacy

AAFC is designed for use by medical professionals and clinics. It is not intended for use by children under 18.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@aafclinic.com
